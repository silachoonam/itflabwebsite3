Superman

Superman is a fictional superhero who originally appeared in American comic books published by DC Comics. The character was created by writer Jerry Siegel and artist Joe Shuster

Batman

who appears in American comic books published by DC Comics. The character was created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger, and debuted in the 27th issue of the comic book Detective Comics on March 30, 1939. Wonderwomen

Wonder Woman is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. The character is a founding member of the Justice League. The character first appeared in All Star Comics #8 published October 21, 1941
